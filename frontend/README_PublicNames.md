**Public Names and Explanations for breeds_AKC_Rsrch_FoodV1 table's variables**

breed_name_AKC - *Breed Name*: The official name of the dog breed according to the American Kennel Club (AKC).

breed_otherNames - *Other Breed Names*: Other names people might use for this dog breed.

breed_group_AKC - *Breed Group*: The group this breed belongs to, including sporting, hound, or toy dogs.

breed_size_categ_AKC - *Size Category*: How big the dog is, like small, medium, large, or extra large based on AKC size categories.

breed_life_expect_yrs - *Life Expectancy by Breed*: How many years this breed usually lives. When available, this number will be research-based. 

listed_DogDiet_MVP - *Available in App*: Whether this breed is included in the current version of the app (Y for yes, N for no). If the app development continues, this list will expand over time. 

food_recomm_brand - *Recommended Food Brand*: The brand of dog food suggested for this breed. Only one food will be recommended in the initial development. If the app development continues, this list will expand over time. 

food_recomm_product - *Recommended Food Product*: The specific product name of the recommended dog food. Only one food will be recommended in the initial development. If the app development continues, this list will expand over time. 
    
food_recomm_format - *Recommended Food Format*: The type of dog food like kibble (dry), wet (canned), and many others. Only one food will be recommended in the initial development. If the app development continues, this list MAY expand over time.
